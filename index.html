"Assignment #2 - AJ Vietvu"

I'm Just Here.

For this assignment, I really just drew what I liked at the time. I was really into drawing skulls, beanies, and trippy looking patterns. For my ART 1 class, we had the opportunity for an assignment to create anything, so I put the three together. This piece was just supposed to be fun and free.

Shoes.

This assignment was for my Photo 40 class. This picture is only one out of a series of four pictures. This series of pictures was just supposed to capture different aspects of fashion and style. Through this picture (and the other three) I wanted to capture the image of what has been popular during today's trends.

Laughing.

This was a portrait assignment for my PHOTO 40 class. Through this photo, I wanted to capture the laughter, happiness, and positivity shown from this individual, naturally. To get this photo, I didn't tell the subjects when I was taking pictures purposely to get the most candid pictures as possible.

Are We Lost?

For this work of art, I challenged myself to create something without any plan whatsoever. I just created shapes and colored everything randomly without any focus.
